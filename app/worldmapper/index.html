Multi depth world map editor by Chris Z.
World map editor with multiple layers of depth. Made by Christoph Z.
* select type of tile and paint on map
* once a tile on the map is selected, use + to zoom in and paint deeper details, use - to unzoom
* tiles which were edited are marked with a red dot. pressing x will clear the selected tile of all details
